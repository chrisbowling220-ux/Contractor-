# BuildPro+ Launch Kit

Your app: **https://builderspro.cc** (live, branded domain — use this everywhere)

**The hook:** **10 free quotes, no card needed** — lead with it everywhere.

Golden rule for all of these: **lead with the time saved and the "look professional"
angle, not the tech.** Contractors don't care that it's AI — they care that it
saves them 2 hours a night and wins them jobs. The "10 free, no card" offer is what
gets them to tap the link; the time-saved story is what makes them stay.

comment, then DM them the link. Post in the MORNING (contractors check phones
over coffee) or after 6pm.

## 3. 30-Second Demo Video Script (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)

The hook in the first 2 seconds is everything. Show, don't tell.

**[0:00–0:03] HOOK — on camera, fast:**
> "Watch me quote this entire bathroom in 30 seconds."

**[0:03–0:10] Show the photo + voice:**
- (Screen recording of the app) Snap a photo of the bathroom.

  surround, new vanity, toilet, and paint."*

**[0:10–0:20] The wow moment — show the result:**
- The material list populates: tile, thinset, grout, vanity, paint, fasteners…

**[0:20–0:27] The payoff:**

- Caption: "POV: you stopped writing quotes by hand 😮‍💨 #contractor #construction
  #handyman #remodel #generalcontractor"
- Post the SAME video to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
- The trades niche LOVES "watch me do X fast" content. This format goes viral.

## 4. The Cold-Start Plan (do this FIRST, before broadcasting)

1. **Get 5–10 contractors you know (or 1 degree away) using it free.**
   - The guy who roofed your house. Your cousin who paints. The framer at your
     supply yard. Anyone who quotes jobs.
2. **Watch them use it.** Note every spot they get confused — fix those.
3. **Collect 2–3 quotes/testimonials.** "Saved me 2 hours a night" sells harder
   than any ad. Screenshot their texts.
4. **THEN** post in groups / make videos with those testimonials baked in.

Word of mouth among contractors is enormous — they all know each other. Five
happy ones become fifty.

## 6. What to charge

Set up and live:
- **Free:** 10 instant quotes to try (no card) — your top-of-funnel hook
- **Pro — $19.99/mo:** unlimited quotes + thank-you letters
- **3 months — $49.99** (~$16.66/mo, saves ~17%) — for people who'd rather pay up front
- **Yearly — $159.99** (~$13.33/mo, best value, saves ~33%) — commitment + cash flow
- **Pay-as-you-go — $1 per quote** (buy 1–10) for the no-subscription crowd

That's a solid spread — less than one hour of a contractor's labor pays for a
month. Don't undercharge: a tool that saves 10 hours/month is easily worth $20.
Push the **yearly** plan to your most committed early users — it locks them in and
gives you cash up front (there are 5 promo codes for 20% off the yearly plan).
